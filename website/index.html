What does Lighthouse do?

Crime Database

We access the local police data from several cities across the United States, creating a collection relevant to users.

Data Presentation

The user is presented with markers on a map, featuring the information from the aggregated crime data. This includes the date and type of crime.

Danger Assessment

Using available data and in-house algorithms, a risk to enter certain areas can be assessed.

Walking Routes
(coming soon)

Given origin and destination information, a walking route based on danger versus effeciency will be given. The priority on danger or speed will be adjustable.
